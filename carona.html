COVID-19

Caronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.

The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough,fever,and in more serve cases,difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently,avoiding touching your face,and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing, but it can also present as a wide range of other symptoms or even be asymptomatic in some cases. The virus can affect individuals of all ages, but it poses a higher risk for older adults and those with underlying health conditions. In response to the pandemic, public health measures such as social distancing, mask-wearing, quarantine, and lockdowns have been implemented to slow the virus's spread and protect vulnerable populations.

How it spreads ?

Caronavirus disease disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneez,it also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it then touches their eyes, nose or mouth COVID-19 primarily spreads through the transmission of respiratory droplets, which are tiny liquid particles expelled when an infected person talks, coughs, sneezes, or simply breathes. These droplets carry the virus and can infect individuals who are in close proximity to the infected person, typically within about 6 feet or 2 meters. The risk of transmission is especially high in indoor settings with limited ventilation.
